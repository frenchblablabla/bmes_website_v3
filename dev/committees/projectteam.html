About

The project team committee is dedicated to the research and development of the BMES project that will be entered in this year's BMEidea. This year we hope to have completed our project and give hands on experience to our team members in the many facets of engineering. We hope to get people involved by providing an exciting project, having hands on project based build days, and allowing members to apply their engineering skills to the best of their abilities.
